## How to Use This Pack

This pack contains 105 prompts organized across 7 categories. Every prompt is copy-paste ready — just replace the bracketed [PLACEHOLDER] variables with your specific details before submitting to your AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

**Tips for best results:**

1. **Replace every placeholder.** Generic inputs produce generic outputs. The more specific you are with your placeholders, the more usable the output.
2. **Chain prompts together.** Use a strategy prompt first, then a writing prompt, then a repurposing prompt. Each builds on the last.
3. **Treat outputs as first drafts.** AI gives you a strong starting point — add your voice, examples, and personality before publishing.
4. **Save your favorites.** Once you find prompts that consistently work for your niche, save the filled-in versions so you can reuse them.
5. **Iterate.** If the first result isn't right, add a follow-up like "Make it more conversational" or "Shorten this to 150 words."

**How placeholders work:**

- `[TOPIC]` — the subject of your content
- `[AUDIENCE]` — who you're writing for (e.g., "beginner freelancers," "busy moms," "SaaS founders")
- `[PLATFORM]` — where this will be published
- `[TONE]` — the voice you want (e.g., "conversational and witty," "professional but warm," "direct and no-fluff")
- `[NICHE]` — your content vertical (e.g., "personal finance," "fitness," "B2B marketing")
