Sales Description

You've built something — a course, a template kit, an ebook, a toolkit. It's good. Maybe even great. But now comes the part nobody prepares you for: the launch. The Digital Product Launch System is the complete operational playbook for creators launching a digital product for the first (or fifth) time. It covers everything from 30 days before launch to 30 days after, including the pre-launch checklist, landing page copy template, a 7-email launch sequence you can customize and schedule in an afternoon, 20 social media announcement templates, pricing strategy worksheet, beta tester feedback forms, launch day operations checklist, post-launch follow-up sequence, KPI tracker, and a lessons learned template.

This isn't theory. It's the actual system — the checklists, emails, posts, and trackers — laid out in order so you know exactly what to do and when. Whether you're launching on Gumroad, your own site, Teachable, or anywhere else, this kit takes the guesswork out of going live and gives you the best possible shot at a successful launch.

What's Inside

  • 30-Day Pre-Launch Checklist — A day-by-day operational checklist covering everything from finalizing your product to building buzz. Organized into weeks: Week 4 (product finalization), Week 3 (audience warming), Week 2 (landing page and email prep), Week 1 (final testing and hype building). Nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Landing Page Copy Template — A fill-in-the-blank landing page structure covering headline, subhead, problem statement, solution, features/benefits, social proof, pricing, FAQ, and CTA. Follows conversion-optimized copy frameworks used by top digital product creators. Includes notes on what to write and common mistakes to avoid.
  • Email Launch Sequence (7 Emails) — A complete 7-email sequence covering the full launch arc: announcement, value/story, social proof, FAQ/objection handling, urgency, last chance, and post-purchase. Each email is fully written with customization brackets. Schedule them and go.
  • Social Media Announcement Templates (20 Posts) — Ready-to-customize posts for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, and multi-platform use. Covers pre-launch teasers, launch day announcements, social proof shares, behind-the-scenes, and final push posts. Just fill in the blanks and post.
  • Pricing Strategy Worksheet — A structured decision-making framework for pricing your digital product. Covers competitor analysis, value-based pricing, anchoring, launch discounts, tier structure, and price testing. Includes a pricing decision matrix.
  • Beta Tester Feedback Form — A template for collecting structured feedback from beta testers before launch. Covers usability, value perception, willingness to pay, missing features, and testimonial collection. Get quotes you can use on your sales page.
  • Launch Day Operations Checklist — A minute-by-minute (okay, hour-by-hour) checklist for launch day itself. Covers final checks, email sends, social posts, community announcements, monitoring, troubleshooting, and celebration. No panic, just process.
  • Post-Launch Follow-Up Sequence (4 Emails) — Emails for after the launch: thank-you/onboarding, testimonial request, referral ask, and evergreen nurture. Keeps the momentum going after launch day energy fades.
  • KPI Tracker — A structured tracker for the metrics that matter during a launch: page views, conversion rate, email signups, sales, revenue, refund rate, and traffic sources. Daily tracking for launch week, weekly tracking for the month after.
  • Lessons Learned Template — A structured post-launch retrospective covering what worked, what didn't, what you'd do differently, and what to try next time. The most underrated document in any launch — fill it out while the details are fresh.
