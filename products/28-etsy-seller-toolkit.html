What's Inside

4 fully-configured databases, pre-linked so your product data flows into orders, keywords inform listings, and competitor insights drive strategy:

  • Products Database — Every listing in your shop with full cost breakdown: materials, labor, Etsy fees, shipping supplies, and actual profit margin. Track listing status, inventory counts, seasonal performance, and identify your highest and lowest margin items at a glance.

Dashboard with linked views showing today's orders to ship, low inventory alerts, top-performing products by margin, keyword opportunities, and revenue tracking.

    FAQ

    Do I need a paid Notion account? The template works on Notion's free plan. A paid plan unlocks unlimited blocks and additional sharing features, but is not required to use the template.

    Is this a downloadable file? No. After purchase, you receive a Notion template link. Click the link, hit "Duplicate," and the entire system is copied to your Notion workspace in seconds.

    Does it automatically sync with my Etsy shop? No. This is a Notion template, not a direct Etsy integration. You enter your data manually or copy it from your Etsy dashboard. The value is in the analysis, organization, and decision-making framework — not automation. Manual entry also forces you to look at your numbers, which most sellers avoid.

    Are the Etsy fee calculations accurate? The template uses Etsy's current fee structure as of 2026: $0.20 listing fee, 6.5% transaction fee, 3% + $0.25 payment processing fee, and optional Offsite Ads fee (15% on orders from Etsy ads for shops under $10K annual revenue, 12% for shops over $10K). You can update these percentages if Etsy changes their fee structure.

    Can I use this if I sell on multiple platforms? Yes. The Products database includes a Sales Channel property (Etsy, Website, Craft Fair, Wholesale, Other). You can track products across channels while keeping your Etsy-specific SEO and competitor data in the dedicated databases.

    What if I get stuck? The quick-start guide covers setup end-to-end. Each database includes example entries to show you how the system works. The template is designed to be self-explanatory.
