Sales Description

There's a reason some people get stunning, consistent results from AI image generators while you're on your fifteenth re-roll hoping for something usable. It's not the tool. It's not luck. It's prompt engineering — and most people are doing it wrong.

Writing "a beautiful mountain landscape at sunset, photorealistic, 4K" is not prompt engineering. It's a description. And descriptions produce generic results. Real prompt engineering is about structure: placing the right descriptors in the right order, using modifiers that image models actually respond to, specifying lighting setups and camera parameters that trigger the rendering engine's trained behavior, and knowing which parameters to adjust for each style and subject.

The AI Art Prompt Engineering Bible is the most comprehensive prompt resource available for visual AI tools. It contains 200+ copy-paste-ready prompts organized across 10 style categories (photorealistic, anime, watercolor, oil painting, 3D render, digital art, pen and ink, cinematic, retro/vintage, and abstract), 8 subject categories (portraits, landscapes, products, architecture, food, fashion, fantasy/sci-fi, and still life), and technique-specific sections covering lighting, composition, camera angles, color palettes, and post-processing effects.

Every prompt is built with the Universal Prompt Formula — a structured syntax tested across Midjourney v5/v6, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion SDXL. You also get a 120+ entry Style Modifier Library, a Camera & Lens Reference Guide, a Lighting Setup Encyclopedia, and platform-specific parameter guides so you know exactly which settings to use on each tool.

This isn't a list of prompts. It's a complete education in visual prompt engineering, packaged so you can use it immediately and learn from it over time.
