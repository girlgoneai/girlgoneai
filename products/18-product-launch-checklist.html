Sales Description

You have a product ready. You have an audience — or you're building one. What you don't have is a clear picture of everything that needs to happen, in what order, by whom, and by when. So you wing it. You go live when you feel ready, post a few times, send one email, and wait. The results are underwhelming. Not because the product is bad, but because the launch was improvised.

The Product Launch Playbook is a comprehensive, battle-tested checklist and timeline system covering every dimension of a modern product launch: technical setup, content creation, email sequences, social media, PR, paid ads, affiliate coordination, and post-launch analysis. It's structured around four phases — from 8 weeks out all the way through 30 days after launch — so you always know exactly what to do next. No guesswork, no last-minute panic, no waking up at 2am wondering if you forgot something critical.

Whether you're a solo creator launching a digital course, a product manager shipping a SaaS feature, or a small team releasing a physical product, this playbook gives you a professional launch operation on day one. It takes the institutional knowledge that experienced launchers build over years of trial and error and puts it in a single document you can start using today.

What's Inside

A complete, ready-to-execute launch system covering every role and every channel:

  • Phase-by-phase checklist — Pre-launch (8 weeks out), Launch Week, Launch Day, and Post-Launch (4 weeks), each broken into specific, actionable tasks
  • ASCII Gantt timeline — Visual overview of the entire launch arc so you can see the big picture and spot scheduling conflicts before they happen
  • Hour-by-hour Launch Day rundown — A minute-level schedule from midnight to midnight so your actual launch day runs like a coordinated campaign, not a fire drill
  • Channel-specific checklists — Dedicated lists for email, social media, PR outreach, paid advertising, and affiliate/partner coordination
  • Launch metrics dashboard template — The exact numbers to track, organized by day, week, and phase, with benchmark ranges for each
  • Post-launch review framework — A structured debrief process to capture what worked, what didn't, and what to carry forward to your next launch
  • Common mistakes guide — The 20 most damaging launch mistakes, why they happen, and exactly how to avoid them
  • Budget allocation template — A percentage-based framework for allocating your launch budget across channels, with guidance for budgets from $500 to $50,000+
  • Quick-start guide — A 15-minute orientation that tells you exactly where to start no matter how many weeks you have until launch
