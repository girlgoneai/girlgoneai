What's Inside

6 fully-configured, pre-linked databases that form a complete pet business management system:

  • Client Database — Every pet parent you work with, with full contact details, address, preferred communication method, how they found you, billing status, and a complete history of every appointment. Rollup formulas surface total revenue per client, last appointment date, and number of pets. Know your best clients at a glance.
  • Pet Profiles Database — Every animal in your care, with species, breed, age, weight, temperament, feeding instructions, medication schedules, vaccination records with expiration dates, vet information, and behavioral notes. Linked to both the client and appointment databases so you always have the full picture before a visit.
  • Appointments Database — Every scheduled service, with date, time, duration, service type, pet(s), client, status, and payment status. Kanban view for your daily schedule. Calendar view for the week ahead. A "Needs Confirmation" view surfaces appointments that haven't been confirmed 24 hours out.
  • Services & Pricing Database — Your full service menu with descriptions, durations, prices, and any add-ons. Linked to appointments so revenue calculations are automatic. Update a price once and it flows through everywhere.
  • Revenue Tracker Database — Every payment received, with date, amount, client, service, payment method, and status (paid, pending, overdue). Monthly and weekly summaries calculate automatically. See your real income — not just what you think you earned.
  • Vaccination & Medical Records Database — Every pet's vaccination history, expiration dates, medication needs, allergies, and vet contact information. A "Vaccines Expiring Soon" view alerts you when a pet in your care needs updated records before their next appointment.
