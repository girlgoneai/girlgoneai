Headline

Running a private practice shouldn't feel like a second job. Get your client roster, session scheduling, billing, and caseload under control — in one organized system built for the way therapists actually work.

Sales Description

You went into private practice to do meaningful clinical work — not to chase down insurance reimbursements, manually reconcile session counts, or dig through scattered notes to find out when a client last attended. But for most therapists, the administrative side of practice is exactly that: a chaotic second job that bleeds into evenings and weekends.

The Therapist Practice Manager gives you a single, organized workspace that covers every non-clinical administrative task: client contact information, session scheduling, attendance tracking, treatment plan milestones, billing and insurance claims, and a high-level caseload dashboard. Everything is connected. When you update a client's session count, their billing record updates. When you log an appointment, your cancellation rate recalculates. No duplicate entry, no spreadsheet-flipping, no end-of-month scramble to reconstruct what happened.

This is not an EHR (electronic health record) or a clinical documentation tool. It does not store clinical notes, diagnoses, treatment content, or any PHI that belongs in a HIPAA-compliant EHR. What it does is handle the operational layer of your practice — the scheduling, billing, and administrative coordination that keeps the lights on — so that when you're in session, you're fully present, not worried about whether that insurance claim went out.

What's Inside

5 fully-configured databases, pre-linked so information flows automatically:

  • Client Roster — Every active and archived client, with contact info, insurance details, session rates, and scheduling preferences. Rollups show total sessions attended, revenue earned, and outstanding balances per client — without any manual math.
  • Session Log — A chronological record of every appointment: date, duration, type, attendance status, and fee collected. Views let you see this week's schedule, a client's full attendance history, or every no-show in the last 90 days.
  • Treatment Plans — Administrative treatment plan tracking: presenting concerns, treatment goals, target session count, review dates, and progress status. Keeps your caseload accountable without storing clinical content.
  • Billing & Insurance Tracker — Log every billing transaction, insurance claim, and client payment. Track claim status from Submitted through Paid, flag outstanding balances, and see exactly what you're owed at any moment.
  • Caseload Dashboard — A live overview of your entire practice: active clients, weekly session volume, monthly revenue, cancellation rate, caseload capacity, and unpaid balances — all in one view.

Pre-built views for every workflow: active client list, weekly schedule, billing pipeline, insurance claim status, outstanding payments, and no-show history.

Key formulas explained so you understand and can adjust them: session utilization rate, revenue per session, cancellation rate, caseload capacity percentage, and insurance reimbursement lag.

Quick-start guide that walks you through initial setup, adding your first clients, and configuring your billing rates — ready for use in under 45 minutes.

Benefits

  • See your entire practice at a glance — The Caseload Dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of active clients, weekly session count, monthly revenue, and open billing items every time you open the system. No more piecing together numbers from different places.
  • Stop losing revenue to billing gaps — Every session you log creates a corresponding billing entry. The Billing Tracker flags every unpaid session, pending insurance claim, and outstanding client balance so nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Track no-shows and cancellations without extra work — Session attendance status flows directly into your cancellation rate formula. You always know which clients are struggling with attendance and can follow up proactively.
  • Stay on top of treatment plan reviews — The Treatment Plans section tracks your next review date for every client and surfaces overdue reviews automatically, so you're never caught off guard before a session.
  • Manage insurance claims without a dedicated biller — The Insurance Tracker gives solo practitioners a structured way to follow every claim from submission through payment, flag aging claims, and identify patterns in denials or delays.
  • Know when your schedule has room — The caseload capacity formula compares your active session count against your target, so you can make confident decisions about taking new referrals, adjusting your hours, or adding a waitlist.

    FAQ

    Is this HIPAA compliant? Can I store session notes in here? No — and you should not. This planner is a practice management organizer for administrative information: scheduling, billing, session counts, insurance claim tracking, and caseload metrics. It is not an EHR and is not designed or certified for HIPAA compliance. All clinical notes, diagnoses, treatment content, and protected health information should remain in your designated HIPAA-compliant EHR or practice management software (e.g., SimplePractice, TherapyNotes, Jane App).

    What kind of billing information can I track here? You can track fee amounts, session dates, payment status, insurance claim numbers, reimbursement amounts, outstanding balances, and claim submission dates. This is administrative billing tracking — the same kind of information you might keep in a spreadsheet — not a formal accounting or claims submission system.

    Do I need a paid Notion account to use this? The template is designed for Notion's free plan. A paid plan provides unlimited blocks and additional sharing features but is not required to get full value from the system.

    Can I use this if I work in a group practice rather than solo? Yes. The Client Roster supports filtering by individual therapist, and you can configure billing rates per client regardless of your practice arrangement. If your group practice uses a shared EHR, this planner works alongside it for your personal administrative tracking.

    Can I customize the fee structures and session types for my practice? Fully. Session types, billing rates, insurance panels, and all Select/Multi-select properties can be edited to match your exact practice. The formula reference section explains each calculation so you can adjust thresholds — for example, changing what counts as "at capacity" for your caseload target — without breaking anything.
