What's Inside

A complete, ready-to-execute podcast launch system:

  • Equipment guide — Mic, headphones, and recording software recommendations at three budget tiers ($50, $200, $500+), with exact product names and why each one matters. No vague "get a good microphone" advice.
  • Hosting platform comparison — Side-by-side breakdown of Buzzsprout, Anchor/Spotify for Podcasters, Libsyn, Transistor, Podbean, and Captivate — pricing, features, distribution, analytics, and a clear recommendation for different use cases.
  • Branding & identity worksheets — Podcast name brainstorming framework, cover art specifications and design brief template, music and intro/outro guidance (including where to find royalty-free options), and a written brand voice guide.
  • Episode planning template — Repeatable structure for solo episodes, interview episodes, and co-hosted episodes. Includes pre-production planning, talking points outline, intro/outro scripts, and post-production checklist.
  • Guest outreach system — Cold outreach email templates, warm introduction scripts, guest research checklist, pre-interview questionnaire, and a guest scheduling workflow. Includes follow-up sequences for non-responders.
  • Show notes template — Copy-paste template for every episode: summary, timestamps, key takeaways, links mentioned, guest bio, and CTAs. SEO-optimized structure for maximum discoverability.
  • Launch day checklist — Hour-by-hour rundown for publishing your first episodes across all platforms, announcing on social media, and executing your launch promotion.
  • 90-day growth strategy — Week-by-week growth playbook covering cross-promotion, social media repurposing, email list building, listener engagement, and review generation.
  • Quick-start guide — A prioritized 30-day timeline so you know exactly what to do each week from today until launch.
