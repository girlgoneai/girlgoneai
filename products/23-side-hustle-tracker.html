Sales Description

Side hustles don't fail because people don't work hard enough. They fail because people don't know their numbers. You're driving for Uber, selling on Etsy, freelancing on the side, and maybe flipping items on weekends — but when someone asks "how much did you actually make last month?" you have to guess.

The Side Hustle Income & Expense Tracker is a fully structured Google Sheets template that handles the financial reality of running multiple income streams alongside a day job. Track income by hustle, categorize expenses, see your real profit per stream, estimate your quarterly tax liability, and get monthly and quarterly summaries that tell you which hustles are worth your time and which are costing you money.

No more end-of-year panic trying to reconstruct what you earned. No more "I think I made money on that." No more guessing at estimated taxes and hoping for the best.

What's Inside

5 interconnected sheets, pre-built and ready to use:

Income Log — Record every dollar earned, tagged by hustle, date, client/platform, payment method, and payment status. Running totals calculate automatically by hustle and by month. Track both gross revenue and net-of-fees income (because platform fees eat into your real earnings).

Expense Log — Track every business expense by hustle, category (supplies, software, marketing, travel, equipment, subscriptions, fees, education, etc.), date, and payment method. Auto-categorization guidance built in so you know what counts as a business expense and what doesn't.

Profit by Hustle — The summary sheet that answers the most important question: what's actually profitable? See gross income, total expenses, net profit, profit margin percentage, and effective hourly rate for each hustle — updated automatically as you log data. Includes a visual ranking so you can see your most and least profitable streams at a glance.

Monthly & Quarterly Summary — Month-by-month breakdown of total income, total expenses, net profit, and estimated tax liability. Quarterly totals with estimated self-employment tax calculations (based on your configured tax rate) so you know what to set aside before quarterly deadlines.

Tax Estimates — A dedicated sheet that calculates estimated quarterly payments based on your self-employment income, your configured federal and state tax rates, and the self-employment tax rate. Includes a payment tracker for logging when you actually make estimated payments and comparing what you owe vs. what you've paid.

Also included: A plain-English setup guide with import instructions, formula activation tips, how to add dropdowns, and a recommended weekly workflow. Plus a template.csv with the complete data structure and sample entries.
