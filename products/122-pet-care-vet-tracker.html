Sales Description

When you have pets, there's a surprising amount to keep track of — vet appointments, vaccination schedules, medication dosages, feeding times, and expenses that somehow add up faster than you expected. Multiply that by two or three pets, and things start slipping through the cracks.

The Pet Care & Vet Visit Tracker is a Notion template built specifically for pet owners who want one organized place for everything. Instead of digging through vet paperwork, trying to remember when the last flea treatment was, or guessing how much you spent on pet food last month, you'll have it all at your fingertips.

Every pet gets their own detailed profile with health records, and the shared dashboards give you a bird's-eye view of upcoming appointments, medication schedules, and expenses across all your animals. It's designed to be quick to update daily and easy to reference when you need information fast — like when the vet asks when the last vaccination was.

Whether you have one spoiled cat or a house full of rescues, this tracker scales to fit your household and keeps you on top of the care routines that matter most.

What's Inside

  • Multi-Pet Profile Pages — Create individual profiles for each pet with their name, breed, birthday, weight history, microchip number, photo gallery, and personality notes. Every pet gets their own dedicated space with all their important information in one view.
  • Vet Appointment Scheduler — Track past and upcoming vet visits with date, reason, diagnosis, treatment notes, and follow-up actions. Set reminders for annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental cleanings so you never miss an appointment.
  • Medication & Supplement Tracker — Log every medication and supplement with dosage, frequency, start/end dates, and refill reminders. See at a glance which pets are on active medications and when prescriptions need to be renewed.
  • Feeding & Activity Logs — Record daily meals, treats, water intake, exercise, and bathroom habits. Customize meal plans per pet and track any dietary changes or sensitivities over time for easy vet reference.
  • Pet Expense Tracker — Categorize spending across food, vet bills, grooming, supplies, insurance, and more. View monthly and yearly summaries to understand your true cost of pet ownership and budget accordingly.
