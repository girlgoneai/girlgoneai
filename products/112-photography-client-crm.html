What's Inside

  • Client Database -- Every client gets a rich profile: contact info, session type, how they found you, communication history, and linked records for their contracts, sessions, payments, and galleries. Search, filter, and sort your entire client history instantly.
  • Session Booking Calendar -- Manage availability, confirm bookings, and track session details (location, time, shot list, special requests). Each session links to the client record so nothing lives in isolation.
  • Contract Tracker -- Log every contract with status indicators (sent, viewed, signed, expired). Set renewal reminders for ongoing clients. Link contracts to specific sessions and see the full paper trail.
  • Gallery Delivery Workflow -- Track each gallery through stages: culling, editing, proofing, client review, revisions, and final delivery. Log revision requests, download links, and delivery dates. Know exactly where every project stands without checking 4 different apps.
  • Payment Tracker -- See deposits, balances, and payment status for every client in one view. Filter by paid, partial, or overdue. Track revenue by month, by session type, and by referral source. Never let an unpaid invoice slip through again.
  • Mood Board Collaboration -- A dedicated space for each client to share inspiration, Pinterest links, color preferences, and styling notes before their session. Walk into every shoot prepared with a clear creative direction.
