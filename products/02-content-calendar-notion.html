    FAQ

    Can I use this for just one platform? Yes. Simply ignore or hide the views and properties for platforms you don't use. The system works just as well for a single-platform creator.

    Does it work for teams? Yes. Notion allows you to share pages with team members, assign tasks using the Person property, and use the Review Needed checkbox for collaboration handoffs.

    What if I publish at a different frequency? The template is not opinionated about how often you publish. You add content at whatever cadence makes sense for your strategy. The calendar view will reflect whatever dates you enter.

    Is there a limit to how many content pieces I can track? No. Notion's database has no practical row limit for this use case. You can track years of content history.

    Do I need the paid Notion plan? The template is fully functional on Notion's free plan. A paid plan unlocks unlimited blocks and guest sharing, which may be useful for larger teams.
