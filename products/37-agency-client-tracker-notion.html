Sales Description

You know the feeling. A client asks for a status update and you spend 20 minutes digging through Slack threads, Google Docs, and half-finished spreadsheets to piece together what's been done, what's in progress, and what's overdue. Your account manager has their version. Your designer has theirs. Your media buyer has a completely different understanding of the timeline. And the client's last email — the one asking about the deliverable you forgot — is still sitting in your inbox unreplied.

The Digital Marketing Agency Client Tracker is a complete agency operations system built natively in Notion. It covers the full client lifecycle: from the moment a new client signs to the monthly report you send them. Onboarding checklists ensure nothing gets missed in the first 30 days. Campaign dashboards track performance across every channel. Deliverables databases show exactly what's been promised, what's in progress, and what's overdue. Retainer management tracks hours and budget against each client's monthly allocation. And reporting templates give you a professional, repeatable format for client-facing updates.

This template was built for the agency that's outgrown spreadsheets but isn't ready for a $500/month project management platform. If you're running 5-30 active clients with a team of 2-15 people, this is the system that keeps everything visible, accountable, and on track.

What's Inside

6 fully-configured, pre-linked databases that form a complete agency management system:

  • Clients Database — Every active, paused, and churned client in one place. Contact details, contract value, retainer terms, start date, account manager, service tier, and status. Rollup fields auto-calculate total deliverables, overdue items, and hours used this month. Each client record is a command center for their entire engagement.
  • Onboarding Pipeline — A Kanban board tracking every new client through your onboarding process: Signed, Kickoff Scheduled, Access Granted, Strategy Call Complete, First Deliverable Sent, Onboarding Complete. Each stage has a checklist of required actions so nothing gets skipped.
  • Campaigns Database — Every campaign across every channel, linked to the client. Track campaign name, platform (Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, Email, SEO, etc.), status, budget, spend, KPIs, start/end dates, and assigned team members. Performance fields capture impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions, CPA, and ROAS. Filter by client, platform, status, or team member.
  • Deliverables Tracker — Every deliverable promised to every client. Name, type (ad creative, blog post, landing page, email sequence, report, etc.), assigned to, due date, status (Not Started, In Progress, In Review, Delivered), and linked to the client and campaign. The "Overdue" view surfaces everything that's past due across all clients in one list.
  • Retainer Management — Monthly retainer tracking per client. Hours allocated, hours used, hours remaining, budget allocation, budget spent, and utilization percentage. Monthly entries create a historical record so you can track trends and justify retainer increases.
  • Meeting Notes Database — Every client meeting documented with date, attendees, agenda, notes, decisions, and action items. Linked to the client record so you have a searchable history of every conversation.

Also included:

  • SOW Tracker — Track every Statement of Work with scope, value, status, and renewal date.
  • Team Allocation View — See who's assigned to what across all clients. Spot overloaded team members before they burn out.
  • Reporting Templates — Monthly and weekly client report templates you fill in and export or share.
  • Dashboard — Agency-wide overview showing total active clients, total monthly retainer value, overdue deliverables, upcoming deadlines, and team utilization.
  • Quick-start guide — Get the system populated and running within one day.

    FAQ

    Do I need a paid Notion account? The template works on Notion's free plan for individual use. For teams, you'll want Notion's Team plan so everyone can edit and collaborate. Notion's Team plan includes unlimited members and blocks.

    Is this a downloadable file? No. After purchase, you receive a Notion template link. Click the link, hit "Duplicate," and the entire system is copied to your Notion workspace. Share it with your team from there.

    How many clients can I track? There's no limit. Notion databases scale to hundreds of entries without performance issues. The template is designed for agencies managing 5-30 active clients but works for any number.

    Can I customize the campaign platforms and deliverable types? Yes — fully. All category fields (platforms, deliverable types, status options, etc.) are Select or Multi-Select properties you can modify in seconds. Add TikTok, remove LinkedIn, rename anything.

    Does this replace project management tools like Asana or Monday? For most agencies under 15 people, yes. It covers client management, deliverable tracking, campaign performance, and team allocation — the core workflows most agencies need. Larger agencies with complex approval workflows or time tracking needs may use this alongside a dedicated PM tool.

    Can I use this for a single client as an in-house marketer? The template is designed for multi-client agencies, but it works well for in-house teams managing multiple campaigns and channels for one brand. Just use the Campaigns and Deliverables databases and ignore the multi-client features.
