Every homeowner starts a renovation with a number in their head. And almost every renovation exceeds that number — by 20%, 30%, sometimes 50% or more — because the budget lived in a spreadsheet that didn't track the right things, or worse, in no spreadsheet at all.

The Home Renovation Budget Tracker is a fully structured Google Sheets template that gives you room-by-room budget tracking, contractor comparison tools, material cost logging, a timeline Gantt view, before/after project notes, a permit checklist, and an ROI calculator for estimating how your renovation impacts resale value. What's Inside

7 interconnected sheets, pre-built and ready to use:

Budget Overview — Your command center. Total renovation budget at the top, with a room-by-room breakdown showing budgeted amount, actual spending, remaining budget, and percentage spent. Color-coded: green when under budget, yellow when approaching, red when over. Summary section shows total project budget, total spent, total remaining, and overall percentage complete.

Room-by-Room Detail — Individual budget sheets for each room/area (kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, living room, exterior, landscaping, general/whole-house). Each room tracks line items: material costs, labor costs, permits, fixtures, appliances, and miscellaneous. Budget vs. actual for every line item. Subtotals per category. Notes column for changes and decisions.

Contractor Comparison — Side-by-side bid comparison for up to 4 contractors per project. Tracks: contractor name, bid amount, timeline estimate, included/excluded items, payment terms, license verification, insurance verification, references checked, and your rating. A weighted scoring system helps you evaluate beyond just price.

Material Cost Tracker — Every material purchase logged with date, vendor, item description, quantity, unit price, total cost, room assignment, and receipt reference. Running total per room. Identifies which rooms are consuming the most materials budget.

Timeline / Gantt View — A visual timeline showing each room/phase with planned start date, planned end date, actual start, actual end, and status (Not Started, In Progress, Completed, Delayed). Shows overlapping phases so you can coordinate contractor schedules. Highlights delays.

Permit & Inspection Checklist — Every permit required for your renovation, with permit type, issuing authority, application date, approval date, cost, inspection date, and pass/fail status. Pre-loaded with common permit types (building, electrical, plumbing, mechanical). Tracks total permit costs.

ROI Calculator — Estimates the return on investment for your renovation based on national remodeling cost-vs-value data. Enter your renovation type and cost, and the calculator estimates the resale value added, ROI percentage, and net gain or loss. Pre-loaded with average ROI data for 15+ common renovation types (kitchen remodel, bathroom remodel, deck addition, etc.).

FAQ

Does this work in Excel or just Google Sheets? The template is optimized for Google Sheets but works in Excel as well. Some conditional formatting may need minor adjustment in Excel.

Can I add more rooms or line items? Yes. The template is fully customizable. Add rows for additional line items, duplicate room sections for additional spaces, or add entirely new sheets for unique project areas.

I'm renovating multiple properties. Can I use this for each? Yes. Duplicate the spreadsheet for each property. House flippers who renovate multiple properties per year find this approach cleanest.

How accurate is the ROI calculator? The ROI estimates are based on national averages from remodeling cost-vs-value studies. Actual ROI varies by market, quality of work, and current real estate conditions. Use it as a planning tool, not a guarantee.

Can I share this with my contractor? Yes. Share specific sheets (like the Timeline) with your contractor using Google Sheets' standard sharing features. You can set view-only or edit permissions per person.
