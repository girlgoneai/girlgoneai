Most couples try to manage this with a combination of spreadsheets, Pinterest boards, wedding apps that spam you with ads, and a notes app that's become a graveyard of half-finished lists. By month three, the "fun" part of wedding planning starts feeling like a second job.

The Wedding Planning Dashboard gives you one connected Notion workspace that handles everything: a detailed budget tracker that shows exactly where every dollar is going, a vendor management system with contracts and payment schedules, a guest list with RSVP tracking and meal preferences, a timeline checklist broken down by months-out milestones, and a seating chart planning tool that makes table assignments actually manageable.

This template was designed by someone who planned a wedding and wished this existed. Every feature solves a real problem couples actually face — not a hypothetical one.

What's Inside

5 fully-configured databases, pre-linked so your budget connects to vendors, your guest list feeds your seating plan, and your timeline keeps everything on track:

  • Budget Database — Every wedding expense categorized and tracked. Set your overall budget, allocate by category (venue, catering, photography, flowers, attire, music, decor, stationery, favors, etc.), track estimated vs. actual costs, log deposits and payments, and see your remaining budget in real time. Built-in formulas calculate category totals, percent of budget used, over/under tracking, and amount still owed per vendor.
  • Vendors Database — Every vendor you're considering, booked, or passed on. Store contact info, quotes, contract status, payment schedules with due dates, review notes, and final ratings. Link vendors to budget items so payments flow automatically into your financial picture.
  • Guests Database — Your complete guest list with RSVP status, meal preferences, dietary restrictions, plus-one tracking, table assignments, gift tracking, and thank-you note status. Rollup formulas give you real-time headcounts by response status, meal choice totals for your caterer, and a clear picture of who still hasn't responded.
  • Tasks Database — A comprehensive wedding timeline broken into phases: 12+ months out, 9-12 months, 6-9 months, 3-6 months, 1-3 months, 1 month, 1 week, and day-of. Every task has a due date, assignee (you, your partner, wedding party, or vendor), priority level, and status. Pre-loaded with 80+ common wedding planning tasks so nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Inspiration Board Database — Save and organize every idea, photo, color swatch, and reference image. Tag by category (flowers, decor, dress, cake, venue styling, table settings) and status (Love It / Maybe / Passed). Link inspiration items to specific vendors or budget categories.

Dashboard views built to give you a wedding command center: budget summary with at-a-glance totals, upcoming tasks and deadlines, RSVP progress tracker, vendor payment schedule, and a countdown to the big day.

Seating chart planning tool with a table assignment system that lets you group guests by table, flag potential conflicts, track dietary needs per table, and see unassigned guests at a glance.

Quick-start guide that walks you from blank template to fully set up in under an hour.

    Is this customizable for our specific wedding? Completely. Add or remove budget categories, modify the task timeline, customize RSVP fields, change table configurations — everything is editable.
