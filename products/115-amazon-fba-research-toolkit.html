What's Inside

  • Product Scoring Matrix -- Evaluate every product opportunity against 10 weighted criteria: monthly demand estimate, number of competitors, average review count, listing quality of top sellers, margin potential, seasonality risk, patent/IP concerns, shipping complexity, improvement opportunity, and brand-ability. Each product gets a total score so you can rank opportunities objectively instead of going with your gut.
  • Profit Calculator -- The most important spreadsheet you'll use. Enter your product cost, shipping, Amazon referral fee, FBA fulfillment fee, storage fees, PPC spend estimate, and target selling price. The calculator shows your per-unit profit, margin percentage, break-even point, and ROI projection. It includes formulas for all current FBA fee structures so you're not manually looking up fee tables.
  • Keyword Tracker -- Log target keywords with estimated monthly search volume, current top-ranking products, relevance score, and suggested PPC bid range. Identify which keywords have high demand but manageable competition -- that's where profitable product opportunities live.
  • Competitor Analysis Dashboard -- Study your top 5-10 competitors for any product: their price, review count, review rating, estimated monthly sales, listing quality score, and main selling points. Spot gaps in the market -- features they're missing, complaints in their reviews, price points they're ignoring.
  • Supplier Comparison Tool -- Evaluate manufacturers side by side: MOQ, unit cost at different quantities, sample cost, lead time, payment terms, communication quality, and compliance certifications. Track the conversation history and sample results so you make sourcing decisions with full context.
