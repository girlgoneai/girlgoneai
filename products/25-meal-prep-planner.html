Sales Description

You know meal prepping saves money. You know it saves time. You know it's healthier. You've probably tried it — pinned 30 recipes on Pinterest, bought containers, prepped one ambitious Sunday, and then never did it again.

The problem wasn't willpower. The problem was the system.

The Meal Prep & Recipe Planner gives you one connected Notion workspace that handles every part of the meal planning workflow: a searchable recipe database with nutrition info and prep times, a weekly meal plan builder that assigns recipes to specific days and meals, and a grocery list system that pulls ingredients from your planned recipes so you buy exactly what you need — nothing more, nothing forgotten.

This template was built for people who want to eat well without turning meal planning into a part-time job. It's simple enough to use in 20 minutes on Sunday, and structured enough to eliminate the "what's for dinner?" panic every weeknight.

What's Inside

3 fully-configured databases, pre-linked so your recipes feed your meal plans and your meal plans generate your grocery needs:

  • Recipes Database — Your personal digital cookbook. Store recipes with ingredients, step-by-step instructions, prep time, cook time, total time, servings, calories per serving, protein/carbs/fat macros, dietary tags, cuisine type, difficulty level, source URL, and personal ratings. Track quantities, check off items as you shop, and link items back to the recipes that need them. Mark pantry staples you already have to avoid rebuying what's in your kitchen.

Dashboard views built to give you a meal planning command center: this week's meal plan at a glance, upcoming prep sessions, favorite recipes quick-access, grocery list for the week, and nutrition summary.

    FAQ

    Do I need a paid Notion account? The template works on Notion's free plan. A paid plan unlocks unlimited blocks, but is not required.

    Can I share it with my partner or family? Yes. Share the Notion workspace with anyone in your household. They can add recipes, adjust the meal plan, and check off grocery items from their own device.

    Does it calculate grocery quantities automatically? The grocery list is a separate database where you add items linked to recipes. You'll enter quantities manually, which gives you control to adjust for what's already in your pantry. The system ensures you know exactly which ingredients you need — you decide how much based on servings and what you have on hand.

    Can I import recipes from websites? You'll manually enter recipes into the database using the recipe template. Most people add recipes as they make them — start with 5-10 favorites and grow from there. The structured format ensures every recipe has consistent, searchable fields.

    What about dietary restrictions? The Recipes database includes dietary tags (Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Keto, Paleo, Nut-Free, Low-Sodium, etc.) and a dietary notes field. Filter your recipe collection by any combination of restrictions to see only what works for your diet.

    Is this a meal delivery service? No. This is a planning tool — you provide the recipes and do the cooking. It organizes your meal planning workflow so you can shop and cook more efficiently.
