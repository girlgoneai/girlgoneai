What's Inside

4 fully-configured databases, pre-linked so your lessons connect to units, students connect to grades, and resources connect to the lessons that use them:

  • Lessons Database — Plan individual lessons with learning objectives, standards alignment, materials needed, differentiation notes, and assessment plans. Status tracking from Draft through Taught with a reflection field to capture what worked and what to adjust next time.
  • Units Database — Map out multi-week units with essential questions, enduring understandings, key vocabulary, pacing timelines, and summative assessments. Every unit links to its individual lessons so you can see the full scope at a glance.
  • Students Database — A complete class roster with contact info, accommodations/IEP notes, behavior log, grade tracking with automatic averages, and parent contact information. Rollup formulas show each student's current standing without manual calculation.
  • Resources Database — A searchable library for every worksheet, video, website, textbook reference, and manipulative you use. Tag by subject, grade level, standard, and resource type so you never lose a great resource again.

Dashboard views built to give you a weekly command center: this week's lessons at a glance, upcoming assessments, students needing attention, recent parent communications, and a unit pacing timeline.

Lesson plan template formatted with the gradual release model (I Do / We Do / You Do) plus space for warm-up, direct instruction, guided practice, independent practice, and closure — customizable to any instructional framework your school uses.

Quick-start guide that walks you from blank template to your first planned week in under 45 minutes.
