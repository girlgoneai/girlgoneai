What's Inside

4 fully-configured, pre-linked databases that form a complete relationship management system:

  • Contacts Database — Every person you know professionally, with full contact details, relationship strength, lead source, tags, and a full history of every touchpoint. Rollup formulas surface how recently you've been in contact and how many open deals are associated with each person. Never forget a name, a context, or a follow-up again.
  • Companies Database — Separate company records linked to all associated contacts and deals. Track company size, industry, annual revenue, website, and status in your pipeline. See total deal value per company and a count of all active engagements at a glance. Useful for agencies pursuing enterprise accounts or anyone managing multiple stakeholders at a single organization.
  • Deals / Pipeline Database — The heart of the system. Every potential or active engagement lives here as a deal card moving through your custom sales pipeline: Lead, Qualified, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Closed Won, Closed Lost. Each deal has a monetary value, a probability, a weighted forecast value (auto-calculated), expected close date, and a full link to both the associated contact and company. Track win rate, average deal size, and total pipeline value without ever opening a spreadsheet.
  • Activities / Touchpoints Database — Every interaction logged in one place. Calls, emails, meetings, LinkedIn messages, proposal sends, follow-up attempts — each one stamped with a date, linked to a contact and deal, and categorized by type and outcome. This gives you a complete relationship timeline for every contact and makes sure no follow-up falls through the cracks.

    FAQ

    Do I need a paid Notion account? The template works on Notion's free plan. A paid plan unlocks unlimited pages and collaborative features for teams, but every database, formula, and view in this template functions fully on the free tier.

    Is this a downloadable file? No. After purchase you receive a Notion template link. Click it, hit "Duplicate," and the entire system — all four databases, all views, all formulas — is copied into your Notion workspace instantly. Nothing to install or configure.

    Can I use this for a small team instead of just myself? Yes. The template works well for teams of two to five. Share the Notion page with teammates using Notion's standard sharing features. For larger teams with more complex permission needs, a Notion paid plan is recommended.

    I already have contacts in a spreadsheet. Can I import them? Yes. Notion supports CSV import into any database. Export your contacts from your spreadsheet and import them into the Contacts database. You'll need to map column names manually, but the process is straightforward and Notion's help documentation covers it step by step.

    What if I want to add custom deal stages or contact categories? Everything is customizable. Deal stages are a Select property — add, rename, or remove stages in seconds. The same applies to contact tags, activity types, lead sources, and every other category in the system. The template is a starting point you own entirely.
