Sales Description

Building a digital product takes weeks or months. Launching it takes... well, most people wing it. They post once on social media, send one email, and wonder why nobody's buying. Then they blame the product. The product isn't the problem. The launch is.

A successful product launch is a sequence of coordinated actions spread across 4-6 weeks: building anticipation before the launch, executing flawlessly on launch day, and optimizing the funnel after the initial buzz fades. Most creators skip steps two and three entirely, and do step one poorly because they don't have a playbook to follow.

The Digital Product Launch Playbook is a complete workflow kit that gives you that playbook. It includes a pre-launch checklist with 45+ action items organized into a 4-week countdown, a launch day timeline with hour-by-hour tasks, email sequence templates covering the full launch arc (announcement through post-purchase), a social media swipe file with 30+ ready-to-customize posts, and a post-launch optimization guide that turns a launch event into an evergreen sales engine.

This is the system that separates "$47 in the first week" launches from "$4,700 in the first week" launches. The difference isn't the product -- it's the execution playbook behind it.

  • Pre-Launch Checklist (45+ Action Items) -- Organized into a 4-week countdown. Week 4: finalize product, set up sales page, configure payment processing. Week 1: final testing, seed reviews or testimonials, prep launch day assets. Every item is a specific action, not a vague suggestion.
  • Launch Day Timeline -- An hour-by-hour schedule for launch day itself. When to send the announcement email. When to post on each platform.
