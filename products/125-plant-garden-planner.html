Sales Description

Whether you have three succulents on a windowsill or a full backyard vegetable garden, keeping track of what each plant needs — and when — is harder than it looks. Different watering frequencies, light requirements, fertilizing schedules, and seasonal timelines mean that even experienced plant parents forget things. And one missed watering week can undo months of growth.

The Plant Care & Garden Planner gives you a structured, beautiful system to manage all your plants in one place. Every plant gets its own profile card with care requirements, and the scheduling tools help you stay on top of watering, feeding, and seasonal tasks without relying on memory alone.

For outdoor gardeners, the seasonal planting calendar helps you plan what to sow and transplant based on your growing zone, and the garden layout planner lets you map out beds, rows, and containers so you make the most of your space. If you're growing edibles, the harvest tracker lets you log yields and track what varieties performed best season to season.

From apartment dwellers nurturing a collection of tropicals to suburban gardeners managing raised beds and fruit trees, this planner scales to fit your green space and keeps everything growing on schedule.

  • Plant Profile Cards — Create detailed profiles for every plant with common and botanical names, species info, light requirements, watering frequency, soil preferences, toxicity notes, and photos. Track repotting history, propagation attempts, and any issues like pests or disease.
