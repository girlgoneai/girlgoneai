What's Inside

8 fully-configured, pre-linked databases that form a complete hosting operations system:

  • Properties Database — Every listing you manage, with address, platform links, max occupancy, bedroom/bathroom count, amenities, wifi details, access instructions, house rules, and key metrics. Rollups calculate total revenue, average nightly rate, and occupancy rate per property. Know how each property performs at a glance.
  • Bookings Database — Every reservation past and future, with guest details, check-in/check-out dates, nightly rate, total payout, platform, booking status, and special requests. Calendar view shows your booking schedule across all properties. A "Gaps" view highlights unbooked periods where you should adjust pricing.
  • Guest Communication Templates — Pre-written message templates for every touchpoint: booking confirmation, pre-arrival instructions, check-in day message, mid-stay check-in, checkout reminder, post-checkout thank you, and review request. Customizable per property. Copy-paste into your platform's messaging.
  • Cleaning & Turnover Schedule — Every turnover linked to a booking, with cleaning team assignment, checklist, status, and time window. The "Today's Turnovers" view shows exactly what needs to happen between checkout and the next check-in. Never miss a cleaning again.
  • Expense Tracker — Every dollar spent on your rental business: cleaning fees, supplies, repairs, maintenance, utilities, insurance, mortgage, platform fees, furniture, and professional services. Categorized by property and expense type. Monthly and annual summaries calculate automatically.
  • Revenue & Financial Dashboard — All income across all properties and platforms, with monthly breakdowns, platform fee calculations, net revenue after expenses, and occupancy rate tracking. Know your actual profit — not just your gross bookings.
  • Review Management Database — Every review received and given, with rating, text, date, guest, and property. Track your average rating over time. A "Needs Response" view ensures you never leave a guest review unanswered. A "Review Request Queue" reminds you to request reviews from guests who haven't left one.
  • Maintenance Log — Every repair, replacement, and improvement, with cost, urgency, status, vendor, and before/after notes. A "Scheduled Maintenance" view surfaces preventive maintenance tasks. Track your total investment per property over time.

Benefits

  • See every property's real performance — Revenue, expenses, occupancy rate, and average nightly rate roll up per property. Stop guessing which listing is actually profitable after expenses.
  • Never miss a turnover — Every checkout triggers a cleaning task with a checklist, cleaner assignment, and time window. The "Today's Turnovers" view is your daily operations center.
  • Communicate professionally without rewriting messages — Guest communication templates cover every touchpoint. Personalize with guest name and property details, then copy-paste. Consistent, professional messaging in seconds.
  • Track real profit, not vanity revenue — The financial dashboard subtracts platform fees, cleaning costs, maintenance, utilities, and every other expense from your gross bookings. Know your actual margins.
  • Maintain your properties proactively — The Maintenance Log tracks every repair with cost and a scheduled maintenance view for preventive tasks. Replace the HVAC filter before it dies, not after a guest complains.
  • Protect and grow your ratings — The Review Management database ensures you respond to every review and request reviews from guests who loved their stay. Your average rating is tracked over time so you can see trends.
