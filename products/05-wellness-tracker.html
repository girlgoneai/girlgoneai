Exercise Log Database Log every workout with type, duration, distance, intensity (1–5 scale), pre and post-workout feelings, PRs, and notes. Mental Health Check-In A weekly mood log with morning and evening ratings, anxiety and stress tracking, and a set of weekly reflection questions about what drained you, what restored you, and what you want more of. Self-Care Menu A curated list of self-care activities organized by how much time you have: 5 minutes, 15 minutes, 30 minutes, or 1 hour. When you're depleted and can't think straight, you don't have to decide — just look at the menu and pick one thing.

Supplement & Medication Tracker Log daily supplements and medications with dose, timing, and notes. Track what you take and build a reference log for medical appointments.
