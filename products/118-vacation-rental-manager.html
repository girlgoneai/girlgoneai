Sales Description

Managing a vacation rental is a juggling act. Guest messages at midnight, cleaning crews that need turnover instructions at 7am, maintenance issues that pop up between guests, and a revenue picture that's scattered across Airbnb payouts, Venmo cleaning payments, and receipts in your email. You started this to earn passive income, but nothing about it feels passive when you're managing everything from your phone's notes app.

The Notion Vacation Rental Manager is a complete operations system for short-term rental hosts. It centralizes your booking calendar with reservation details and guest profiles, includes guest communication templates so you're not rewriting the same check-in instructions every week, provides a turnover cleaning checklist your cleaning team can follow without texting you questions, tracks revenue and expenses so you actually know your profit margins (not just your gross payouts), and manages guest reviews with response templates and improvement tracking.

Whether you manage one cozy cabin or a portfolio of 5+ properties, this system turns the operational chaos of hosting into a repeatable, professional process. Your guests get a better experience. You get your evenings back.

  • Guest Communication Templates -- Pre-written messages for every stage: booking confirmation, pre-arrival details (Wi-Fi, parking, access codes), check-in welcome, mid-stay check-in, checkout reminder, and post-stay thank-you with review request.   • Cleaning Checklist -- Room-by-room turnover checklist your cleaning crew can access directly.   • Review Management -- Track every review: platform, rating, guest name, key feedback, and your response. Response templates for 5-star reviews, 4-star reviews, and (inevitably) the occasional 3-star. An improvement tracker logs recurring feedback so you can fix the issues guests keep mentioning.
